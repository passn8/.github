### Manufacturing -- Passn8.io

*The Brain atop your IIoT stack.* We leverage Data Science and Agentic AI to turn raw industrial data into autonomous operational intelligence.

**Foundation:** Universal Connectivity and Edge Analytics for real-time local response. Built on **EdgeX Foundry** at the edge, with seamless integration into **Rockwell Automation** (FactoryTalk), **PTC** (ThingWorx, Kepware), **AWS**, and **Azure** platforms.

At the heart of our offerings lies Passn8.io -- a cutting-edge IIoT application service that empowers businesses with real-time insights and actionable data. With its adaptability and ease of integration, clients can quickly leverage our technology to enhance operations and stay ahead in their industries.
